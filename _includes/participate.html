We are recruiting for our research studies

We are always seeking native speakers of Dutch to participate in our studies. Currently in 2021, in addition to Dutch, we are running studies in Mandarin Chinese, French, and Turkish.
In our brain studies, we typically use a brain monitoring technique called magnetoencephalography, or MEG, to record very small magnetic activity in the brain. It poses no known health risks. Our studies usually require a time commitment of one to two hours and can be scheduled at a time that is convenient for you. As compensation, we provide â‚¬10 per hour for your time.

Contact

To register your interest, the best way to reach us is to email us at lacns@mpi.nl. Please also state the following:

- your age, - whether you have any non-removable metal, such as permanent piercings, medical implants, and dental work such as braces/retainers, - whether you have any (history of) neurological disorders, - whether you have normal, or corrected to normal, hearing and vision, - any other languages you speak and at what proficiency.
